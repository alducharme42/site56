The Mingan Archipelago National Park Reserve

The Mingan Archipelago National Park Reserve is a wonderful place to visit. Outdoor enthusiasts will be delighted with hikes through the many islands. The national park is distinguished by the presence of gigantic monoliths. During your expedition, it is possible to observe whales, seals and several seabirds such as the Atlantic puffin. During the period from April to January, it is possible to take the ferry and discover the marvelous landscapes of Anticosti Island.

Sports and activities

Thanks to the Gulf of St. Lawrence which runs along the city, Havre Saint-Pierre is the perfect place for lovers of kayaking and other water sports such as kite surfing and scuba diving. You can go on an expedition and discover the many islands in the surroundings. The territory surrounding Havre Saint-Pierre is also popular for hunting and fishing. There are salmon rivers and trout lakes. Several excursions are organized during the year.

Photo Credit: Canada Parks

Food and Culture

If you want to learn more about the history of the municipality of Havre Saint-Pierre, the Maison de la culture Roland-Jomphe is the ideal place. In addition to a warm welcome, you will learn all about the history and highlights from 1857 to today. There is a stage and several activities for children. It is also possible to have a guided tour by the staff. Just one kilometer from the Maison de la culture, you will be able to taste the maritime products of the North Coast at the Chez Julie restaurant. This one has been an emblematic place in Havre Saint-Pierre since 1977. Fish and seafood lovers will be delighted.
