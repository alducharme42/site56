About Us

Tourisme Havre Saint-Pierre collects all the activities and events that take place in the municipality and in the surrounding area. If you are passionate about the sea and like to go on expeditions, Havre Saint-Pierre is the perfect place for you. Alone or with your family, you will be amazed by the beauty of the Mingan Archipelago landscape. For all food lovers, you will be delighted with freshly caught products from the region. Take the opportunity to discover the culture of the Cayens at the Maison de la culture Roland-Jomphe. The latter occupies a very important place in the history of the city.
